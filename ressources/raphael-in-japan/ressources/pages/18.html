Maid cafés (メイド喫茶 or メイドカフェ) are a subcategory of cosplay restaurants found predominantly in Japan. In these cafés, waitresses, dressed in maid costumes, act as servants, and treat customers as masters (and mistresses) as in a private home, rather than as café patrons.
