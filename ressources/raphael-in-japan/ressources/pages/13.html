The TeamLab Borderless Digital Art Museum was a permanent digital art exhibition in the Mori Building in Odaiba, Tokyo. The museum was an audiovisual creation by the TeamLab art collective of animators, programmers, architects, mathematicians and designers. Note: TeamLab Borderless is currently closed and will be relocated in 2023.
